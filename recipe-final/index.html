kimchi fried rice

INGREDIENTS

3 bowls of steamed rice

1 cup of chopped kimchi

1 green onion, chopped

3 teaspoons of sesame oil

2-3 tablespoons of gochujang

1 sheet of seasoned seaweed

DIRECTIONS
Step 1.
Heat up the pan and stirfry kimchi with vegitable for 1 minute.
Step 2.
Add rice, kimchi juice, water, and gochujang. Stir all the ingredients together for about 7 minutes.
Step 3.
Add sesame oil and remove from the heat.
Step 4.
Sprinkle with chopped green onion, roasted seaweed, and sesame seeds.
Serve right away.
